ABOUT JELLE

The Artist Behind the Lens

"I believe every woman deserves to experience herself the way the people who love her most already see her."

I created Silhouette and Soul because I believe every woman deserves to experience herself the way the people who love her most already see her — radiant, powerful, and completely herself.

My private studio in West Kelowna is a space where you can arrive as you are and leave with photographs you will return to for the rest of your life. Every session is a collaborative journey, stripping away the noise of daily life to focus on the quiet power of your unique silhouette.

Through years of mastering fine art techniques and light manipulation, I've dedicated my craft to creating a sanctuary where vulnerability meets strength. This isn't just about taking pictures; it's about seeing you.

THE STUDIO

Behind the Process

Excellence is found in the unseen details. Every portrait produced in this studio is treated as a piece of archival history, crafted with an unwavering commitment to longevity and tactile beauty.

We choose only the finest museum-grade papers and custom-built lighting environments to ensure that the depth of your soul is translated perfectly onto the final medium. It is an intentional, slow process designed to honor the significance of your journey.
