THE EXPERIENCE

What safety looks like in my studio

Every session at Silhouette & Soul begins with a simple requirement: bring someone you trust. A partner, a friend, a sister. Whoever makes you feel safest. They're there with you from the start, because this kind of work deserves a witness you've chosen yourself.

Before we begin, we talk through everything. Wardrobe, comfort levels, what you're hoping to walk away with. You're in control at every moment. Want to pause, adjust, or skip something? Say the word. No explanation needed.

I've designed every part of this experience — the lighting, the pacing, the reveal — so that you feel held, not exposed. The goal is always the same: that you leave feeling more like yourself than when you arrived.
